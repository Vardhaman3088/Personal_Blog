About Me

I am an Head Of Department at information Technology in the Department of Information Technology at Yeshwantrao Chavan College of Engineering (YCCE),Nagpur.I have worked for 17 years Yeshwantrao Chavan College of Engineering as a Professor out of which 7 years I work as head of Department in Yeshwantrao Chavan College of Engineering (YCCE). My PhD was Rashtrasant Tukdoji Maharaj Nagpur University,I completed My M-tech from VNIT nagpur and B-tech from VJTI mumbai ,Ihave a work experiance of 1 years.The problems I study usually have two distinct aspects: an algorithmic aspect and a societal aspect. A common thread in my research lies in collecting and analyzing large scale data from online systems to understand whether there is any cause for concern, and then develop mechanisms to eliminate or reduce the impact. My research agenda is inherently interdisciplinary, and therefore, the tools and theories that I often use in my works come from machine learning, network science, media studies, computational social choice, social welfare and behavioral economics.
